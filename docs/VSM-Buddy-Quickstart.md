# VSM Buddy — Quick Start (one page)

*Map a process, find the waste, and walk away with an automation proposal — in about 30 minutes.*

## Before you sit down (10 min at the process)

Walk the process once, start to finish, and jot down for each step:

| Write down | Example |
|---|---|
| Step name | "Assess & quote" |
| Work time — hands-on minutes per item | 30 min |
| Queue — items waiting in front of the step right now | 12 |
| Right-first-time % — how often there's no rework | 85% |
| Anything that annoys people | "requests arrive missing info" |

**Estimates are fine.** A map that's 80% right today beats a perfect one next month.

## In the app (15 min)

1. Open **Value Stream** in the menu → **New map** (or **Load sample** to see a finished one first)
2. **Tab 1 · Learn** — skim it once, it explains the ideas in plain English
3. **Tab 2 · Collect Data** — type in what you wrote down.
   *Shortcut:* click the blue **"describe the process in your own words"** bar and let the AI draft the steps for you, then correct them.
   Check the **"Does the data look believable?"** panel at the bottom before moving on.
4. **Tab 3 · Map** — the picture draws itself; the red step is your bottleneck
5. **Tab 4 · Action Plan** — copy the **Plain-English summary** straight into an email to your manager
6. **Tab 5 · Scope of Works** — answer five quick questions and click **Generate**: you get a ready-to-discuss proposal for automating the process with Microsoft 365 tools. **Download for Word** or **Print/PDF** to share it.

## Rules of thumb

- One map = one process (e.g. "work order processing", not "the whole company")
- Count the queues — waiting time, not work time, is almost always the biggest waste
- Map *what actually happens*, not what the procedure says should happen
- Found something confusing? Use **Send feedback** in the sidebar.
